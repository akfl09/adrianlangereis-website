About Adrian

Built From
Experience

I've done it all in this industry — led a top-producing team, built and operated my own boutique brokerage, and earned my Managing Broker's license in 2013. Today I serve as Managing Broker for Real Broker BC, bringing a hands-on approach to every transaction.

As a current member of both the Victoria Real Estate Board and the Greater Vancouver Real Estate Board, I bring deep local knowledge across two of BC's most dynamic markets. When you work with me, you get the experience of $800 million in closed deals — applied directly to yours.
